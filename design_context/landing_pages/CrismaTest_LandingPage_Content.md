## Section 8 — FAQ

**Section label (pill)**
> FAQ

**Headline (H2)**
> Frequently asked questions

**Q1 — How accurate is the test?**
> CrismaTest combines six evaluation dimensions — logic, communication, job skills, behavioral indicators, anti-fraud consistency, and optional video — to produce a holistic score with significantly higher predictive validity than traditional CV screening or single-skill tests.

**Q2 — How long does it take?**
> Most candidates complete the full test in 10 to 15 minutes. The adaptive format means the test adjusts to your responses — no two sessions are identical.

**Q3 — How is cheating prevented?**
> CrismaTest uses real-time AI proctoring that monitors webcam activity, tab switching, typing patterns, copy/paste behavior, audio environment, and secondary screen usage. Any anomalies are flagged and included in the candidate's TrustScore breakdown.

**Q4 — Can companies customize the test?**
> Yes. Companies on Starter, Pro, and Enterprise plans can use the Test Builder to select specific modules, adjust question focus, and add up to 3 custom questions — all without any technical configuration.

**Q5 — Can I reuse my CrismaScore?**
> Absolutely. Your CrismaScore is valid for 12 months from the date of your last test. You can share it via a unique profile link, embed it on LinkedIn, or send it directly to any employer — anywhere in the world. Free plan candidates receive 2 free retests.

## Notes d'implémentation (pour Pencil)

### Wording rules
- Tous les titres en sentence case (pas de Title Case sauf le nom de marque)
- Boutons primaires : toujours actionnable (`Start`, `Get`, `Try`) — jamais passif (`Learn more`)
- Éviter les superlatifs vagues (`best`, `amazing`) — préférer les chiffres (`10x faster`, `70% reduction`)

### Tone of voice
- Confiant mais pas arrogant
- Data-driven — chaque claim a un chiffre
- Direct — phrases courtes, verbes actifs
- Premium — jamais de ton startup décontracté excessif

### i18n notes
- Toutes les strings ci-dessus correspondent aux clés EN dans `/locales/en.json`
- Les traductions FR doivent respecter le même niveau de registre (professionnel, direct)
- Les badges de score (`LogicScore`, `CrismaScore`, `TrustScore`) restent en anglais dans les deux langues — ce sont des noms de produit

### CTA hierarchy rules
- Une seule CTA primaire (filled) par section
- Maximum une CTA secondaire (outline ou ghost) par section
- Ne jamais mettre deux CTAs primaires côte à côte
- Hero exception : deux CTAs autorisées (candidat vs entreprise = deux audiences)
