- **Mobile nav**: Bottom tab bar 56px, brand-navy, 5 icon-only tabs — sidebar absent on mobile
- **Seeded data**: Marie Dupont (Score 78), James Chen (Score 65), Sofia Torres (Score 91), Omar Hassan (Score 55)
- **Status badges**: Reviewed (green), Pending (amber), Invited (brand-primary)
- **Skeleton shimmer**: E2E8F0 bg rectangles simulating loading rows/cards
